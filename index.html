Biography

Tony Stark, born to Howard and Maria Stark, was a child prodigy and engineering genius. Following in his father's footsteps, Tony became the CEO of Stark Industries, where he revolutionized technology and weapons development. After being captured and forced to create a weapon of mass destruction, Tony instead built the first Iron Man suit and used it to escape. This marked the beginning of his transformation into Iron Man, a hero dedicated to using his technology for good.

With a sharp intellect and unmatched resources, Stark has continuously upgraded his suits, making him one of the most formidable superheroes in the world. His journey is one of redemption, responsibility, and the relentless pursuit of innovation.
