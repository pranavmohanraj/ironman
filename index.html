Biography

Iron Man is the alter ego of Tony Stark, an industrialist and inventor who creates a powered exoskeleton to save his life and later uses it to protect the world. His suit is equipped with advanced technology, weapons, and flight capabilities, making him one of the most powerful heroes in the Marvel Universe.
